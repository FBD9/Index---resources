NOTE:   MD5 checksum tool is called md5sum
    	SHA-1 checksum tool is called sha1sum
    	SHA-256 checksum tool is called sha256sum

In terminal, type:

For sha256: "sha256sum filename.filetype"

For sha1: "sha1sum filename.filetype"

For MD5: "md5sum filename.filetype"

Then take the given hash and compare.
